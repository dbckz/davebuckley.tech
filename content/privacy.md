### Technical Implementation

This privacy-first approach is achieved through:

- **Static Site Architecture** - This site is built with Hugo, a static site generator. There is no server-side processing, no database, and no user sessions to track.

- **No JavaScript Analytics** - No Google Analytics, Matomo, Plausible, or any other analytics platform. Your visit is not counted, timed, or analyzed.

- **No Cookies** - This site sets no cookies. Your browser storage remains untouched.

- **Minimal External Resources** - Only essential web fonts are loaded. No advertising networks, social media trackers, or third-party scripts.

- **HTTPS Only** - All connections are encrypted. Your browsing is protected from eavesdropping.

- **No CDN Tracking** - Hosted on GitHub Pages without additional CDN tracking layers.

## Why Privacy Matters

As someone who works professionally on privacy-enhancing technologies and data governance, I believe in practicing what I preach. The web has become a surveillance apparatus, but it doesn't have to be.

Every website you visit that tracks you contributes to a detailed profile of your interests, behaviors, and movements across the internet. This site refuses to participate in that ecosystem.
